# Ferns-And-Petals-Sales-Analysis (Interactive Dashboard using MS Excel)

## Project Objective
Ferns and Petals (FNP) wants to analyze their sales data across different occasions, product categories, and cities. So that, the business team can understand revenue patterns, customer spending behavior, and optimize product offerings and delivery performance for growth.

## Dataset Used
- [Dataset](https://github.com/Git-Salomi/Ferns-And-Petals-Sales-Analysis/blob/main/Ferns%20%26%20Petal%20Sales%20Analysis.xlsx)

## Questions (KPIs)
- What is the Total Revenue generated across all orders?
- What is the Average Customer Spending per order?
- What is the Average Order-to-Delivery time?
- Which months generated the highest revenue?
- Which product categories contribute the most to revenue?
- Which occasions (Diwali, Holi, Birthday, etc.) drive the most sales?
- Which are the top 10 cities by number of orders?
- What is the revenue distribution across different order hours?
- Which are the top 5 products by revenue?

## Dashboard Interaction
- [View Dashboard](https://github.com/Git-Salomi/Ferns-And-Petals-Sales-Analysis/blob/main/Ferns%20%26%20Petals%20Sales%20Analysis.png)

## Process
- Verified data across Customers, Orders, and Products sheets for missing values and inconsistencies.
- Made sure data is consistent and clean with respect to data types, date formats, and category values.
- Created pivot tables according to the KPIs asked.
- Merged all pivot tables into one dashboard and applied slicers to make it dynamic.

## Project Insight
- Total revenue of ₹35,20,984 was generated across 1,000 orders with an average spend of ₹3,520 per order.
- August and February are the highest revenue months, driven by Raksha Bandhan and Valentine's Day respectively.
- Colors and Soft Toys are the top-performing product categories by revenue.
- Anniversary and All Occasions contribute the most to overall sales among all occasion types.
- Imphal, Kavali, and Dhanbad are among the top cities by number of orders.
- Evening hours (18–21) show the highest order volumes, suggesting peak buying time.
- Average order-to-delivery time is approximately 5.53 days.

## Final Conclusion
To boost Ferns and Petals' revenue, marketing efforts should focus on high-performing occasions like Anniversaries, Holi, and Raksha Bandhan with targeted promotions. Colors and Soft Toys categories should be stocked and highlighted prominently. Evening time slots should be leveraged for flash sales and push notifications. Additionally, improving delivery speed in top-ordering cities like Imphal and Dhanbad can enhance customer satisfaction and repeat purchases.
